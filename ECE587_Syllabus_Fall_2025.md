## Course Description
This course deals with fundamental building blocks (sub-circuits) of analog integrated circuits and the design of analog integrated circuits using SPICE as the simulation tool.

## Course Schedule (Tentative)
The following topics will be covered. The schedule is tentative and may change based on the flow of classroom discussions.

1. **CMOS Device Modeling:** Large-Signal and Small-Signal MOS Transistor Models (First Week)
2. **Differential Amplifiers:** (2nd and 3rd week)
3. **Passive and Active Current Mirrors:** (4th and 5th week)
4. **Operational Amplifiers:** (6th to 9th week)
   - *Project – I due in week 8*
   - *Exam – I in 9th week*
5. **Noise:** (10th week)
6. **Stability and Frequency Compensation of Amplifiers:** (11th and 12th week)
7. **Bandgap References:** (13th week)
8. **Switched-Capacitor Circuits:** (14th week)
   - *Project – II due*
